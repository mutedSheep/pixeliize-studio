About Us

Welcome to Pixeliize Studio!

Professional Multimedia, Made Simple.

Your go-to platform for high-quality, accessible digital content creation.

At our core, we believe that professional design and digital storytelling should not be limited by high costs or complex software curves. Our mission is to provide an organized, all-in-one centralized system where individuals, content creators, and small businesses can easily access custom editing services and ready-to-use digital templates. Whether you need rapid photo enhancements, professional video production, or a flexible monthly subscription to keep your branding consistent, our platform is designed to seamlessly deliver professional visual standards with maximum efficiency.

Our Core Commitments

Bridging Cost and Quality

Our goal is to provide a highly competitive, budget-conscious ecosystem with flexible pricing tiers, making elite multimedia production genuinely accessible to students, startups, and local enterprises.

Centralized Creative Efficiency

We eliminate the exhaustion of switching between unverified freelance marketplaces and fragmented self-service applications by integrating full-scale human creativity and automated assets into a single hub.

Standardized Quality Assurance

Every custom request and digital asset undergoes a rigorous, managed workflow and quality control check. This strict oversight ensures that our outputs deliver the exact professional consistency needed to build long-term client trust.

Empowering the Digital Economy

Beyond providing services, we champion digital literacy and local creative collaboration. We strive to support content creators with the essential visual tools, trending resources, and modern templates required to confidently build their brands in a fast-paced market.

At Pixeliize Studio, we are here to handle your digital multimedia workflow — not just to complete a design project, but to become a reliable partner in your brand's growth and digital evolution.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date (Last Updated): May 30, 2026

Pixeliize Studio respects your right to privacy and complies with the provisions of Republic Act No. 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012). This Privacy Policy explains how Pixeliize Studio collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal data upon accessing our platform and using our multimedia, editing, and digital template services.

1 Data We Collect

We collect the following information when you log into and use the Pixeliize Studio platform:

  • ▸
    Full Name — To personalize your account dashboard, process service invoices, and manage client relations.
  • ▸
    Email Address — For sending order confirmations, account notifications, and delivering digital design assets.
  • ▸
    Phone Number — To contact you regarding urgent project updates, custom video/photo editing requirements, or subscription issues.
  • ▸
    Password — Encrypted credentials to secure your account and ensure restricted access.
  • ▸
    Media Uploads — RAW files, footage, audio, and images submitted by you for our one-time or subscription editing services.
  • ▸
    Session Cookies — To maintain a seamless login session while browsing templates or managing your active design orders.
  • ▸
    Reviews and Feedback — Design evaluations and ratings you share after a project is completed.

2 How Pixeliize Studio Uses Your Data

  • ▸Facilitate account registration, secure access, and subscription management.
  • ▸Process, fulfill, and track your digital template downloads and multimedia editing requests.
  • ▸Verify client identities, communicate project timelines, and deliver high-quality media outputs.
  • ▸Maintain cloud-based project histories to allow continuous revisions within subscription tiers.

3 Data Sharing and Infrastructure

Pixeliize Studio strictly protects your data. Your private media files and personal information will NOT BE SOLD OR SHARED with unauthorized third parties, except under the following conditions:

  • ▸
    Cloud Infrastructure Providers — Secure data processing and cloud hosting via Microsoft Azure.
  • ▸
    Legal Obligations — Compliance with law enforcement requests, court orders, or automated tax compliance under BIR guidelines.

4 Technical Safeguards and Data Security

Pixeliize Studio implements robust security measures to protect your digital workspace from unauthorized access, leaks, or cybersecurity threats:

  • ▸
    Password Hashing — Passwords undergo non-reversible cryptographic hashing, remaining hidden even from platform administrators.
  • ▸
    Database and Code Integrity — Protection against script-based attacks (XSS) and SQL injections to protect user databases.
  • ▸
    Storage Security — All uploaded media files, design drafts, and custom templates are stored securely with server-side authentication protocols.

5 Your Rights as a Data Subject

In accordance with the Data Privacy Act, you retain full ownership of your data. You have the right to request access to your uploaded files, correct inaccuracies in your account details, or demand the complete erasure of your project history from our active servers.

6 Account Deletion and Retention

  • ▸
    Account Deletion — Users may request to permanently delete their account at any time, which purges all active personal credentials from our platform database.
  • ▸
    Project Retention — Completed project files and templates may be archived temporarily for a limited period to allow subscription-based re-downloads, subject to terms of service.

7 Children's Policy

Pixeliize Studio's B2B and freelance digital services are intended for individuals who can legally enter binding contracts. We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact our support team immediately.

8 Cookies and Analytics

We utilize session and performance cookies to optimize platform loading speeds, remember your workspace preferences, and gather anonymous analytics regarding template popularity.

9 Updates to This Privacy Policy

Pixeliize Studio reserves the right to modify this policy as our digital services expand. Users will receive an email notification regarding significant changes, and the most current version will always remain accessible on our official web application.

10 Contact Us

For any questions, data inquiries, or privacy concerns, you may contact the Pixeliize Studio management team at pixeliizestudio@gmail.com.
